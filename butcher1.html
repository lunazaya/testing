Whole pig – Bulk order of A WHOLE PIG. This side of pork is custom butchered for you by our butcher. A cut sheet will be sent to you prior to butchering for you to choose your cuts. Cost for a side is $7/lb + butcher fee which is generally between $200-$250 ($1.25/lb and additional for sausage making and smoking) based on instructions. ESTIMATED WEIGHT PER PIG is 200 lbs HANG WEIGHT - however can range from 175- 225lbs. A $400 deposit is taken at the time for a pork share to confirm order. This will be deducted from the final amount owing. Estimated space requirement for a side of pork is 7-8 cubic feet. Pork is packed in butcher paper, labelled and frozen at the butcher.
